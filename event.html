GOONJ

Goonj is the annual techno-cultural fest of University Institute Of Engineering And Technology, organized by students of the college under faculty guidance every year. The spirit of the fest is to promote cultural as well as technical alacrity among the students. Moreover, the motto is to spread zeal among students, relieving them from the stressful schedule of an engineering atmosphere. The fest includes cultural activities like singing, dancing, poetry, acting, along with technical competitions like hackathons.

LEGACY

Legacy is the flagship event of BPC (BRAND PROMOTION COMMITTEE), held every year in the month of November. It is a 2-day-long mini fest consisting of numerous technical and cultural activities and competitions wherein students from all nearby colleges come to enjoy the joyous environment amidst the examination season. Various performances are showcased by the cultural committees of the college, adding more to the vibrant atmosphere.
