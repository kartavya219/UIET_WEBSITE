MECHANICAL DEPARTMENT

U.I.E.T.

VISION

"To be Fountainhead of Technological solutions for Needs of Society and Industry."

Mission

  1. To impart fundamental engineering skills & knowledge for analysis of engineering problems.
  2. To engage with Industry/Society for taking up stimulating problems on merits.
  3. To take the project execution to “Minimum Usable Prototype (MUP)” stage/Pilot-testing and secure IP rights.

CORRE VALUES

  • 100% coverage of syllabus by faculty.
  • Actual delivery of around 40 lectures for a subject.
  • Delivery of lecture for around 50 minutes by a faculty in a lecture class of one hour.
  • 100% engagement of Tutorials.
  • Presence of faculty in the lab during entire time of lab class.
  • Regular checking of practical files by faculty.
